Although it only got 4G storage and 512M RAM, the respond speed is not slow,
and given that it has wifi, I can use it to work online in many places.

All hardware inside works fine, including camera, card reader, suspending,
brightness, volume control, video out, suitable for presentation as well.

The weight is nearly nothing, under 1kg including battery.

But it is quite hot, about 45'C I think.
