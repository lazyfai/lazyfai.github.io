--- layout: post title: sed tips - compressing space date: '2008-05-13T22:41:00.002+08:00' author: Fai Wong tags: - work modified_time: '2008-05-13T22:44:35.436+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8503500.post-168600188006390587 blogger_orig_url: http://lazyfai.blogspot.com/2008/05/sed-tips-compressing-space.html --- Assume in.txt contains an output of dpkg -l, for example:
ii zip 2.32-1 Archiver for .zip files
To compress the line to single space character in between the columns, we can use this sed pattern for replacing extra spaces to one space:
cat in.txt | sed -e "s/[ ][ ][ ]*/ /g"
This will replace the consecutive spaces to one space.
