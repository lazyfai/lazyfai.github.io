For people who got more than one computer (or more than one OS), one of the problem is the bookmarks in all of the browser profiles, foxmarks is a good tool to synchronize the bookmarks on all of you computer / OS, I used it for several months, the performance is good and accurately synchronized all of my bookmarks, now I am using the beta version of foxmarks 2.0 already.
