## For my reference, here is the layout of the Kedei 3.5 inch LCD for Raspberry Pi

### [Kedei LCD Website][kedei-site]

| Kedei LCD Pin | RPi Pin | RPi Pin Name |
|---------------|---------|--------------|
| GND           | 20      | GND          |
| IRQ           | 22      | GPIO25       |
| T_CS          | 24      | GPIO08       |
| L_CS          | 26      | GPIO07       |
| MOSI          | 19      | GPIO10       |
| MISO          | 21      | GPIO09       |
| CLK           | 23      | GPIO11       |
| GND           | 25      | GND          |

[kedei-site]: http://www.kedei.net/
